LetterSlides

Welcome to LetterSlides!

Build your vocabulary knowledge solving scrambled 8-letter words and reward yourself by learning the meaning of each word puzzle you complete. Designed to be simple and minimal, this sliding puzzle is perfect for those who want a fun and casual game to play.

LetterSlides is on the App store!

Highlights

  • Over 16000 words with definitions gathered from the free dictionary API.

  • Random shuffling algorithm ensures puzzles are not shuffled the same way.

  • Built in Unity which supports multi-platform builds.
